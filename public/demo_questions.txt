INSTRUCTIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Select the correct "Bulk Format" in the dashboard before uploading.
2. Ensure your file contains ONLY the question type you selected.
3. Use the pipe symbol (|) to separate the parts of your question.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[FORMAT 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (MCQ)]
Select "Format: MCQ" in the dashboard.
Format: Question Text | Option A | Option B | Option C | Option D | Correct Letter | Points

Example:
What is the capital of France? | London | Berlin | Paris | Madrid | c | 1
Which planet is known as the Red Planet? | Earth | Mars | Jupiter | Venus | b | 2
HTML stands for? | Hyper Text Markup Language | High Tech Modern Language | Hyper Transfer Mode Link | None | a | 1

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[FORMAT 2: TRUE / FALSE]
Select "Format: T/F" in the dashboard.
Format: Question Text | True or False | Points

Example:
The sun rises in the west. | False | 1
Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius. | True | 1
Python is a programming language. | True | 1

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[FORMAT 3: DESCRIPTION]
Select "Format: Desc" in the dashboard.
Format: Question Text | Points

Example:
Explain the theory of relativity in simple terms. | 5
Describe the process of photosynthesis. | 3
Write a short essay on the importance of education. | 10
